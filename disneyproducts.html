All About the Disney shops

The first Disney Store opened in the Glendale Galleria, Glendale, California on March 28, 1987. In April 1990, a location opened in Montclair, California with the first Mickey's Kitchen fast food restaurant with 25,000 opening day visitors.

Facts about the Disney products/ stores


  • With more than 330 Disney Store locations worldwide, complemented by DisneyStore.com, an enchanting Disney shopping experience is available 24 hours a day
  • Locations that still have Disney stores open after most of them closed are ohio, south carolina, oregon, New York, Nevada, New Jersey and Texas
  • By the time Disney opened its first store in China in 2015, there were more than 200 Disney Stores in North America, more than 40 in Japan and more than 70 Disney Store locations in Europe. It currently operates six resorts and 12 parks across the world.
  • Disney's global consumer products operations include the world's leading licensing business across toys, apparel, home goods, digital games and apps; the world's largest children's print publisher; Disney store locations around the world; and the shopDisney e-commerce platform.
  • The Walt Disney Company's retail footprint includes the global shopDisney e-commerce platform and shopping experiences in Disney Parks, lifestyle and outlet locations, stores and third-party retailers around the world, including Disney store shop-in-shops at select third-party retailers.
